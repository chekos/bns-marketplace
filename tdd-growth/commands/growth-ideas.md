When /growth-ideas is used with:

$ARGUMENTS

Generate growth ideas and experiment proposals based on the context provided.

## Step 1: Understand Context

Analyze the provided context to understand:
- Current growth stage and priorities
- Specific area of focus (if mentioned)
- Constraints or preferences
- Recent performance (if shared)

If no context is provided, ask about:
- Current subscriber count and growth rate
- Main acquisition channels
- What's been tried before
- Resource constraints

## Step 2: Generate Ideas

Create ideas across multiple categories:
- Referral program optimizations
- Partnership opportunities
- Content-driven growth
- Social media tactics
- Viral mechanics

## Step 3: Prioritize and Detail

Use ICE scoring (Impact, Confidence, Ease) to prioritize.
Provide detailed implementation plans for top ideas.

## Idea Categories

When generating ideas, consider these categories:

### Referral & Viral
- Referral program design/optimization
- Share triggers and mechanics
- Viral content formats
- Word-of-mouth accelerators

### Partnerships
- Newsletter cross-promotions
- Content collaborations
- Guest appearances (podcasts, newsletters)
- Co-created resources

### Content-Driven
- SEO-optimized pillar content
- Lead magnets
- Content upgrades
- Trending topic coverage

### Social Amplification
- Thread series
- Engagement tactics
- Community building
- Influencer engagement

### Conversion Optimization
- CTA improvements
- Landing page optimization
- Onboarding improvements
- Retention tactics

## Notes

- Prioritize ideas that match current resources and stage
- Include both quick wins and longer-term investments
- Make ideas specific and actionable
- Reference growth-tactics skill for detailed playbooks
