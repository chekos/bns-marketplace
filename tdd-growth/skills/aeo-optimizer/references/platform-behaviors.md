# AI Platform Citation Behaviors

How different AI assistants find and cite sources.

## ChatGPT (with browsing)

- **Source preference**: Authoritative, comprehensive, well-structured
- **Citation style**: Links at end or in footnotes
- **Recency bias**: Moderate (values evergreen + recent)
- **Format preference**: Long-form, detailed explanations
- **Key trigger**: Being the most comprehensive source on a topic

## Perplexity

- **Source preference**: Recent, specific, directly answering queries
- **Citation style**: Inline citations throughout response
- **Recency bias**: Strong (93% of citations from 2024-2025)
- **Format preference**: Listicles, numbered guides, clear structure
- **Key trigger**: Having the exact answer to a specific question

## Google AI Overviews

- **Source preference**: Top 10 organic ranking pages
- **Citation style**: Cards linking to sources
- **Recency bias**: Inherits from organic ranking
- **Format preference**: Matches traditional SEO signals
- **Key trigger**: Ranking well organically first

## Optimization Priority

For tacosdedatos, prioritize:
1. **Structure first**: All platforms reward clear formatting
2. **Direct answers**: Lead with the answer, explain after
3. **Freshness signals**: Update dates, current year mentions
4. **Topic authority**: Multiple interlinked articles on subject
