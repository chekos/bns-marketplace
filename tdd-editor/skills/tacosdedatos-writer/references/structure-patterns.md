# Structure Patterns

## Top 3 Post Structures

### Structure A: The Transformation Arc (Most Common)

- **Opening: 4-Beat Rhythm (150-200 words)**
  - Beat 1: Spark - personal moment of realization (1-2 sentences)
  - Beat 2: Stakes - why this matters now (2-3 sentences)
  - Beat 3: Zoom out - universal truth/context (1-2 sentences)
  - Beat 4: Thesis forward - where we're going (1 sentence)
- **Section 1:** "Lo que cambió" / "What Changed" - contrast before/after with specific details (300-400 words)
- **Section 2:** Practical tools/methods - concrete THEN abstract (500-700 words)
- **Section 3:** "Caja de Acción" / actionable steps (200-300 words)
- **Closing:** Reframe (not recap) + CTA (50-100 words)

### Structure B: The Deep Dive Tutorial

- **Opening: 4-Beat Rhythm (100-150 words)**
  - Beat 1: Spark - counterintuitive discovery
  - Beat 2: Stakes - problem's real cost
  - Beat 3: Zoom out - industry/broader implications
  - Beat 4: Thesis forward - promise of solution
- **Section 1:** Context with personal stake + specific metrics (200-300 words)
- **Sections 2-4:** Numbered deep dives - always ground abstractions in code/examples (1500-2000 words)
- **Section 5:** "Lo Que Descubrí" / key revelations (300-400 words)
- **Closing:** Reframe as new possibility - "Tu Primera Acción" (100-150 words)

### Structure C: The Reflective Analysis

- **Opening: 4-Beat Rhythm through scene (200-300 words)**
  - Beat 1: Spark - vivid sensory moment
  - Beat 2: Stakes - emotional/philosophical weight
  - Beat 3: Zoom out - connect to universal experience
  - Beat 4: Thesis forward - journey we'll take
- **Body:** 3-5 observations, concrete details before abstractions (800-1200 words)
- **Philosophical insight** - grounded in specific examples (300-400 words)
- **Closing:** Return to opening scene but reframed, not summarized (150-200 words)

## Opening Formulas

### Formula 1 - Achievement Spark
- Spark: "Esta semana alcancé un nuevo nivel de [specific achievement]"
- Stakes: "Lo que antes tomaba [X tiempo] ahora toma [Y tiempo]"
- Zoom: "Estamos en un momento donde [universal shift]"
- Thesis: "Te voy a mostrar exactamente cómo [promise]"

### Formula 2 - Problem Spark
- Spark: "Tengo una confesión: [counterintuitive admission]"
- Stakes: "[Specific cost/pain] que todos enfrentamos"
- Zoom: "El problema real no es [X], es [Y]"
- Thesis: "Aquí está lo que descubrí después de [specific experience]"

### Formula 3 - Scene Spark
- Spark: "[Time], [place], [sensory detail that matters]"
- Stakes: "En ese momento entendí [realization]"
- Zoom: "Como [broader group], todos [universal challenge]"
- Thesis: "Esta es la historia de cómo [transformation]"

## Closing Formulas (Reframing, not recapping)

- "Mañana por la mañana, [new possibility]. La pregunta no es [old frame], es [new frame]"
- "Empieza con [simple action]. Pero prepárate: [unexpected consequence]"
- "Todo esto no es sobre [what they think]. Es sobre [reframe]"
- "La próxima vez que [common situation], recuerda: [new lens]"

## Transition Phrase Toolkit

- **After concrete examples, before abstractions:** "Pero aquí está lo profundo...", "Lo que esto realmente significa..."
- **Building momentum:** "Pero la diferencia real es...", "Pero nota qué es diferente..."
- **Grounding abstractions:** "Por ejemplo, [specific case]...", "En la práctica, esto se ve así..."
- **Adding layers:** "Mientras tanto...", "Además de eso...", "Más importante..."
- **Shifting perspective:** "Ahora bien...", "Desde otro ángulo..."
- **Revealing insights after detail:** "Lo que descubrí...", "Resulta que..."
- **Creating urgency:** "Esto no es solo...", "El problema real es..."
- **Connecting ideas:** "Como dice [expert]...", "De manera similar..."

## Optimal Post Length

- **Sweet spot:** 1,200-1,800 words (7-10 minute read)
- **Tutorial/deep dive posts:** 2,000-2,500 words with clear section breaks
- **Reflection pieces:** 800-1,200 words with poetic language
- **Quick insights:** 600-800 words with one clear takeaway

## Key Structural Patterns

- Always establish stakes within first 150 words
- Ground every abstraction with specific detail/example
- Include visual breaks (images/code) every 300-400 words
- End with reframe, never summary
- Posts with 3-5 main sections + action items perform best
- Concrete-to-abstract flow in each section for clarity
